# Compliance And Disclaimers

This file is the practical compliance starting point for future Talley Wealth content and deliverables. The existing [Compliance Content Workflow](../compliance-content-workflow.md) remains the deeper reference.

## Working Rules

- Do not imply guaranteed tax savings, investment returns, or legal outcomes.
- Do not present estate or tax strategies as final legal or tax advice.
- Do not overstate certainty when the right answer depends on client facts, tax law, legal documents, underwriting, or outside professionals.
- Keep testimonial and review usage tied to public review themes, not guaranteed future experience.
- Client-facing generated drafts require David review before delivery.

## Preferred Disclaimer Pattern

Use a humanized disclaimer where possible:

For discussion purposes only. This is not individualized legal or tax advice. Estate, tax, and investment decisions should be reviewed against the full facts and with the right professional before implementation.

## Tooling Rule

Any generator that uses Slant or Drive should mark drafts as review-required and should surface source limitations in the internal section.
