# Talley Wealth SEO Strategy

## Objective
Win local search first, then expand into niche authority beyond geography.

The strategic position is not generic financial advisor. It is David-led, planning-first, tax-integrated financial advice for people whose decisions have become too connected to handle one piece at a time.

## Current Operating Priority

For the next 30 days, treat the site as an assurance and monitoring project after the technical cleanup deploys. The primary growth engine should shift back to repeatable video/content, with the website acting as the trust hub and search foundation.

The highest-priority audience for new content is pre-retirees, especially around retirement timing, Roth conversions, retirement income, Medicare/Social Security, and tax-aware withdrawal planning.

## Best-Fit Market
- Pre-retirees and retirees with meaningful assets.
- Business owners with tax complexity.
- Executives and professionals with equity compensation.
- Physicians and Ballad Health employees.
- Eastman employees and retirees.
- People whose CPA, advisor, investments, taxes, and estate plan feel disconnected.
- People who want a thinking partner, not just portfolio management.

## Core Differentiation
Talley Wealth is a financial planning firm that starts with world-class CFP-level planning, then continues the work through ongoing planning, investment management aligned to the plan, and optional tax preparation. The unusual part is not that each service exists. The unusual part is that they are coordinated under one planning system.

## Brand Architecture
- David Talley: expert voice, educator, trust builder, local authority.
- Talley Wealth: firm, process, team, continuity, fiduciary structure.
- Keystone Method: proprietary planning framework.
- Talley Tax: proof of tax depth and implementation capability.

## Geographic Priority
1. Johnson City: primary home market and first local SEO win.
2. Bristol TN/VA: second priority because of personal roots and family/local credibility.
3. Kingsport: third priority, especially Eastman-related content.
4. Tri-Cities regional authority: consolidate Johnson City, Bristol, and Kingsport into a broader regional footprint.
5. Knoxville and Asheville: keep pages live, improve gradually, but do not center the first campaign there unless business development actively supports it.
6. Beyond local: expand through niche topical authority, not more city pages.

## Ranking Strategy
Local SEO should reinforce relevance, distance, and prominence:
- Relevance: city/service/persona pages that match the actual client problem.
- Distance: clear local presence, address, service areas, office page, local language.
- Prominence: reviews, citations, backlinks, video presence, author/entity strength, local partnerships.

Authority building should begin selectively: local publications, professional/community mentions, podcast or workshop appearances, directory/citation cleanup, and repurposed David-voice articles from strong videos. Do not turn this into an aggressive backlink campaign unless indexing is healthy but impressions/rankings remain stuck after the watch period.

## Success Metrics
- Google Business Profile calls, direction requests, profile interactions.
- Search Console impressions/clicks for priority local queries.
- Rankings for Johnson City, Bristol, Kingsport query clusters.
- Form submissions and phone calls from organic landing pages.
- Growth in branded searches for David Talley and Talley Wealth.
- Video topics that become indexed pages and generate search impressions.
