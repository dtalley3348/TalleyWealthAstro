# Video-Led Content Engine

## Strategic Advantage
David is comfortable recording short-form and medium-form educational videos. That should become the raw material for SEO, AI visibility, social reach, YouTube search, Google Business Profile posts, email, and blog content.

## Core Workflow
One recorded answer becomes:
- YouTube title, description, chapters if long enough.
- Instagram/short-form caption.
- Website article or FAQ page section.
- Internal links to relevant service, city, and persona pages.
- Google Business Profile post.
- Newsletter/email snippet.
- Compliance archive item.

## Best Video Formats
- Question and answer: 30 seconds to 3 minutes.
- Short story: 60 seconds to 5 minutes.
- Whiteboard explanation: 2 to 10 minutes.
- Myth correction: 30 to 90 seconds.
- Localized answer: 45 seconds to 3 minutes.

## Content Pillars
1. Planning-first advice.
2. Retirement income and tax coordination.
3. Roth conversions and tax windows.
4. Business owner financial planning.
5. Eastman/Ballad/local employer benefits.
6. How advisory relationships should work.
7. Stories about disconnected advice and what better coordination looks like.

## Publishing Cadence
Recommended starting point:
- 1 strong search-intent answer per day.
- Optional 1-2 lighter clips per day if quality stays high.
- 1 weekly longer video or whiteboard explanation.
- 2-3 website articles per week created from the strongest videos.

Do not chase volume at the expense of clarity. For search and AI, the reusable, well-structured answer matters more than posting frequency alone.

## Example Recording Prompts
- What is the difference between tax preparation and tax planning?
- How much should I convert to Roth before retirement?
- Why does retirement planning change five years before you retire?
- What should Eastman employees think about before choosing pension options?
- Why can two advisors and a CPA still leave gaps?
- What does Talley Wealth do differently after the plan is built?
- When is Keystone overkill?
- How should a business owner think about selling in the next five years?

## Website Conversion Pattern
Each article created from a video should include:
- Short direct answer near the top.
- David byline and credentials.
- Local or client-fit context when relevant.
- 3-5 internal links.
- Compliance-safe disclaimer.
- CTA to an Explore Call or relevant Keystone page.
