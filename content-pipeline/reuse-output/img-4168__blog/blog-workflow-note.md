## Source Lines

- True retirement readiness is not just about financial security. It requires asking what life will look like when the career ends, and many people have never been asked—or answered—that question.
- Many people reach retirement age without thinking deeply about what their life will actually look like or what will give them purpose and satisfaction beyond work.
- Can I retire, and how do the numbers work?
- Retirement planning is more than just numbers. What do you want life to be after work?
- Most retirement planning is about the numbers. But almost no one stops to ask: what do you actually want your life to be after work?
- Retirement is the first time many people realize they never planned for purpose. Numbers are the easy part. Monday is the hard part.
- The biggest risk in retirement may not be running out of money. It’s running out of meaning.
