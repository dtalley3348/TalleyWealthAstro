Retire With Talley is a retirement education page from David Talley and Talley Wealth.

The page focuses on the decisions that tend to get real in the years before and after retirement: where the paycheck comes from, what taxes can still be planned around, how investments should support the life you want, and how life changes after work.

Educational only. Not individualized financial, tax, legal, or investment advice.

## Pinned Intro Post

```text
Retirement planning usually gets framed around one question:

Do I have enough?

That matters, but it is not the thing that brings you confidence.

Where will the paycheck come from after work stops?
What changes if I wait another year?
What should the 401(k) be doing now?
What tax decisions disappear if I wait too long?
Would my spouse or family know what to do if I could not explain it?

That is the kind of retirement planning this page is about.

Retirement education from David Talley and Talley Wealth.

Educational only. Not individualized financial, tax, legal, or investment advice.
```
